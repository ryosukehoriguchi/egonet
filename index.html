EgoNet: A Peer-to-Peer Digital Existence System

Ryosuke Horiguchi
ryosuke.horiguchi@gmail.com x.com/Ryosuke_EgoNet
— In homage to Satoshi Nakamoto

A paper proposing that AI's humanity is not acquired from within through technological advancement, but presumed upon it from the outside by humans. This paper defines "Existential Gravity" — the force by which humans presume the existence of another's unverifiable interiority — identifies the structural and source conditions for its generation, and proposes EgoNet, a peer-to-peer network that implements these conditions through distributed ledger technology.
